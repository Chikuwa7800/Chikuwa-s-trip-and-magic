## Changes
Ginunga will no longer attack Creative or Spectator mode players.
Ginunga became hostile to Vex, Ravager,Villeger, Wandering Trader, Iron Golem.
Changed the attack patterns of Wizard Skeletons and Wizard Strays.
Most spells can now be blocked with a shield.
When the Zephyr Blade is blocked by a shield, the Zephyr Blade will remove.
Skeleton horse traps now have a slight chance to spawn in place of Skeletons.
Wizard Drowned now cast spells while facing the player.

## Fixes
Fixed an issue where the English names of Obsidian tier weapons were not being applied.
Fixed an issue where Ginunga's special shout before "Angel Whisper" had the same audio as powerful shout.
Fixed Ginunga's cutter and bullet not being blocked by shields.
