## Additions

Added new items: Grave Heart, Ginunga Soul, and Slice of Lament.

## Changes

Changed the model for spell books in the GUI and item entities.
All additional item models have been changed from custom model data to item model.
Changed the appearance of all level 2 spell books.
The colors of bolt-element items, effects, and particles have been unified with the color of spell books.
Spell can now be cast using only spell books.
Changed the textures of the knife, spear, glaive, magic core, frenzied wakame core, seaweed shard, ten times compression wakame, and wakame sword.
The entity control range of glaives has been reduced by 0.5.
Changed base items of some items.
The rarity of the frenzied wakame core has been increased to rare.
The rarity of the empty spell book has been decreased to common.
